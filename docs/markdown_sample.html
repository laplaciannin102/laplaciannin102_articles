線形計画問題 不等式標準形

  • Preview


$$ \begin{aligned} \left \Vert \quad \begin{aligned} &\textbf{min.} \quad &&\sum_{j=1}^{n} c_{j} x_{j} \in \mathbb{R} \\ \\ &\textbf{s.t.} \quad &&\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} x_{j} \geq b_{i} \, (i = 1, \cdots , m) \\ &&&x_{j} \geq 0 \, (j = 1, \cdots , n) \end{aligned} \right. \end{aligned} $$

  • Markdownでの記法
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left \Vert \quad
\begin{aligned}
&\textbf{min.} \quad
&&\sum_{j=1}^{n} c_{j} x_{j} \in \mathbb{R} \\
\\
&\textbf{s.t.} \quad
&&\sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} x_{j} \geq b_{i} \, (i = 1, \cdots , m) \\
&&&x_{j} \geq 0 \, (j = 1, \cdots , n)
\end{aligned}
\right.
\end{aligned}
$$



単体法 辞書

  • Preview


$$ \begin{aligned} D &= \left( \begin{array}{c:c} 0 & C ^\mathrm{T} \\ \hdashline -B & A \end{array} \right) \\ \\ &= \left( \begin{array}{c:ccccc} 0 & c_{1} & \cdots & c_{j} & \cdots & c_{n} \\ \hdashline -b_{1} & a_{11} & \cdots & a_{1j} & \cdots & a_{1n} \\ \vdots & \vdots && \vdots && \vdots \\ -b_{i} & a_{i1} & \cdots & a_{ij} & \cdots & a_{in} \\ \vdots & \vdots && \vdots && \vdots \\ -b_{m} & a_{m1} & \cdots & a_{mj} & \cdots & a_{mn} \\ \end{array} \right) \\ \end{aligned} $$

  • Markdownでの記法
$$
\begin{aligned}
D &= \left(
\begin{array}{c:c}
0 & C ^\mathrm{T} \\
\hdashline
-B & A
\end{array}
\right) \\
\\
&= \left(
\begin{array}{c:ccccc}
0 & c_{1} & \cdots & c_{j} & \cdots & c_{n} \\
\hdashline
-b_{1} & a_{11} & \cdots & a_{1j} & \cdots & a_{1n} \\
\vdots & \vdots && \vdots && \vdots \\
-b_{i}  & a_{i1} & \cdots & a_{ij} & \cdots & a_{in} \\
\vdots & \vdots && \vdots && \vdots \\
-b_{m} & a_{m1} & \cdots & a_{mj} & \cdots & a_{mn} \\
\end{array}
\right) \\
\end{aligned}
$$
